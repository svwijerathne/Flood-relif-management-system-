Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a request?

Login to your account and navigate to the 'Request Relief' page. Fill in your district, family size, and the type of aid required.

What happens after I submit?

Your request is sent to our administrators and categorized by severity. You can view the status in the 'View Requests' section.
